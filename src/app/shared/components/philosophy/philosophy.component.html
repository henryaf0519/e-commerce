Our philosophy

Altitude is an Ingredient.

Zapatoca isn’t just our home; it’s our flavor profile. Nestled in the high Andes, our unique "Silk Climate" allows us to cool our sweets naturally, preserving the integrity of the ingredients.

We don't use "sugar" as a filler. We use Panela—evaporated cane juice that retains the minerals and complex flavors of the land. This isn't just candy; it’s the edible history of the Colombian mountains.
